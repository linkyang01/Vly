# Body Scan Guide (10 minutes)

## Speed Requirements
- Speed: Very slow (recommend 0.5x)
- Pause between sentences: 3-5 seconds
- Tone: Soft, soothing, warm

---

## Text (copy to TTS website)

Find a comfortable position to lie down. Close your eyes gently. Breathe naturally. Let your body completely relax.

Bring your attention to your right foot toes. Feel each toe. Now relax your toes slowly. Feel warmth rising from the soles. Move toward the right ankle. Completely relax.

The left foot as well. Both feet are very relaxed.

Bring your attention to your right calf. Feel the calf muscles. Let them completely relax. The right thigh. Muscles slowly loosen. The left leg as well. Both legs feel like cotton.

Bring your attention to your abdomen. Feel the rising and falling with each breath. With every exhale, the abdomen completely relaxes. Feel the warmth.

Now the chest. Following the breath. With every inhale, the chest slightly expands. Exhale, completely relax. Feel the heartbeat rhythm.

Bring your attention to the right arm. Fingers, palm, forearm. Completely relax. The right arm feels very heavy. The left arm as well. Both arms are very relaxed.

Shoulders slowly relax backward. Feel the connection with the neck. Let gravity take away all tension.

Finally, the entire face. Forehead relaxes. Eyebrows spread. Eyes completely relaxed. Cheeks, chin. The entire face is peaceful like a baby.

Now your entire body is very relaxed. From feet to the top of the head. Completely relaxed. Just lie quietly and feel. Give yourself a moment. Slowly return to the present. Gently open your eyes.
