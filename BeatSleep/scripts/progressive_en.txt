# Progressive Muscle Relaxation Guide (10 minutes)

## Speed Requirements
- Speed: Very slow (recommend 0.5x)
- Pause between sentences: 3-5 seconds
- Tone: Soft, soothing, warm

---

## Text (copy to TTS website)

Find a comfortable and quiet place to lie down. Close your eyes gently. Let your body completely relax. Feel each breath.

Now, bring your attention to your feet. Feel the warmth at the soles. Slowly tighten your toes. Hold. Release. Feel the relaxation.

Move your attention to your calves. Tighten the calf muscles. Hold. Completely release. Feel the weight sinking into the ground.

Bring your attention to your abdomen. Gently tighten the abdominal muscles. Feel the tension. Breathe out. Let everything relax.

Now your arms and shoulders. Slowly make fists. Feel the tension in your forearms. Release. Let your shoulders completely relax.

Finally, the facial muscles. Tighten your forehead. Release. Clench your jaw. Release. Let your entire face completely relax.

Now your entire body is very relaxed. Feel the rhythm of your breath. Just lie quietly. Give yourself a moment. Slowly return to the present. Gently open your eyes.
