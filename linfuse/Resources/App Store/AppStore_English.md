## App Description (English - US Market)

### Short Description (160 chars max)

**linfuse** - Smart video library manager for macOS & iOS. Organize, sync, and enjoy your video collection anywhere.

### Extended Description

**linfuse** is your ultimate video library management solution for macOS and iOS. Transform how you organize and enjoy your personal video collection with powerful features designed for media enthusiasts.

**🎬 Key Features:**

**📁 Smart Library Organization**
- Automatically scan and organize videos from local storage and network drives (SMB/NFS/WebDAV)
- Support for all popular video formats: MP4, MKV, MOV, AVI, WMV, and more
- Multi-folder library management with automatic detection

**🎨 Beautiful Metadata Integration**
- Automatic metadata fetching from TMDB (The Movie Database)
- Beautiful movie posters, backdrops, and artwork
- Cast & crew information, plot summaries, and ratings
- Smart genre categorization and recommendations

**▶️ Premium Viewing Experience**
- Built-in video player with hardware acceleration
- Multiple quality options and subtitle support
- Continue watching across all your devices
- Playback progress sync via iCloud

**☁️ Seamless iCloud Sync**
- Your library, everywhere - sync across Mac and iPhone/iPad
- Watch progress syncs automatically
- Favorites and playlists available on all devices
- Conflict-free data synchronization

**❤️ Track What You Watch**
- Detailed watch history with timestamps
- Smart "Continue Watching" collection
- Create custom favorites collections
- Rate and review your favorites

**📱 Designed for All Apple Devices**
- Native macOS app with optimizations for M-series chips
- iPhone and iPad apps with touch-friendly interface
- Dark mode support across all platforms
- Universal purchase - one purchase for all devices

**👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Sharing**
- Subscribe once, share with up to 5 family members
- Everyone gets their own personalized experience
- No additional setup required

**💎 Subscription Plans:**

- **Monthly**: $1.99/month
- **Yearly**: $9.99/year (58% savings)
- **Lifetime**: $19.99 one-time purchase
- **Free Trial**: 7 days free on all plans
- Cancel anytime

**Privacy First**
- Your data stays yours - stored locally and synced via iCloud
- No tracking, no ads, no data collection
- Offline-first design
